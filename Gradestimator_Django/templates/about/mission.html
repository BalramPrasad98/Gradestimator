- Our Mission -

To leverage the power of big data to help student find their perfect courseload and acheive academic success

Our Product:

At Gradestimator, we believe that students should be equipped with the knowledge necessary for them to succeed in the classroom, and that this process starts before they purchase textbooks. Our goal is to provide students with a realistic idea of how they will perform in a given course based on their performance in previous courses.

Utilizing analytics, our product helps students understand how they will perform in a given course based on their course history and the academic records of students who performed similarly.

How it works:



Step 1:
A student selects a course that they would like the Gradestimator to generate a prediction for.
Step 2:
Utilizing data for all students the Gradestimator identifies courses that may serve as a good predictor for the student's selected course.
Step 3:
The student inserts their grades for these related courses into the system
Step 4:
And now for the magic! Based on the information provided by the student, the Gradestimator identifies other students who took the course grade to be predicted as well as had a similar course history. Based on these students the Gradestimator will provide a recommendation on how it believe the student will perform in the course
