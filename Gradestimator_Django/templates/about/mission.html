- Our Mission -

To leverage the power of big data to help student find their perfect courseload and acheive academic success

How it works:

At Gradestimator, we believe that students should be equipped with the knowledge necessary for them to succeed in the classroom, and that this process starts before they purchase textbooks. Our goal is to provide students with a realistic idea of how they will perform in a given course based on performance in their prior courses. This is done through analytics, where we identify other students who have similar backgrounds, and use their performance to provide an accurate and apt prediction.
